PERFORM (P): PS-max has no dock space for pads, so perform is a full-screen PERFORM ROOM takeover — pads + macros over a dimmed canvas. Cleanest stage, but you lose sight of layers while playing.

OPERATORS (⌘⇧I): modal routing canvas — PS-max keeps modulation out of the chrome entirely; the only always-visible traces are the ⌁ badges in Layers.
