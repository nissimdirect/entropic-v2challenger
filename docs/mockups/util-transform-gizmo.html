The Photoshop free-transform grammar, built once as util.transform — reused by every future tap-transform.

Extends BoundingBoxOverlay.tsx (today: move/scale/rotate for clip transforms only) with a skew drag-mode and the full Photoshop modifier grammar (decision ㊳, banked).
